Why Share The Ride?

It's a fast, fun, affordable way to commute. While carpooling isn't new, Share The Ride is a fresh way to share the road and the cost of commuting. A cost effective alternative to rideshare services, Share The Ride lets you choose to drive or ride with people already goning your way.
